Magnolia is a largegenus that contains over 200 flowering plants species. It is named after French botanist Pierre Magnol, and having evolved before bees appeared theflowers were developed to encourage pollination by beetles.

Some magnolias, such as Magnolia stellata and Magnolia soulangeana, flower quite early in the spring before the leaves open. Others flower in late sring or aerly summer, such as Magnolia grandiflora.
